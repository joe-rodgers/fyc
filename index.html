FYC.WTF

FYC.WTF is a voucher service that works with customers to deliver Bitcoin on their terms. Our approach is simple, keep it simple and private.

Each customer has unique needs so we put a huge focus on customer service. We value privacy and use ecrypted communication channels whenever possible with customers. We use industry leading voucher provider Azteco to fulfill the technical side of business.

  • FYC.WTF is fast // Topping up with Azteco takes only a few seconds both to issue and redeem a voucher.
  • FYC.WTF is simple // No account is needed to redeem an Azteco Voucher. Everyone already knows how to use it!
  • FYC.WTF is safe // Azteco doesn't have user accounts, just like Pay as You Go SIM Cards. It's "disposable Bitcoin" for small amounts.

Bitcoin changes everything.

Bitcoin is a secure and safe way to send money to anyone anywhere in the world instantly. It completely eliminates payer fraud. All you need is a wallet and you can immediately start sending and receiving payments from anywhere world-wide, without waiting, without forms to fill, without fees to receive, and no middle man.

Bitcoin is to money as email is to hand-written post cards on paper sent by post. Bitcoin completely simplifies and greatly speeds up how you send money to other people, and Azteco makes getting Bitcoin easy.

FYC.WTF brings a concierge type experience to buying Bitcoin. We make sure you have a safe buying experience andflexible payment options.

Vouchers are simple

Once our communications are secured and your order is placed, we create an Azteco voucher that includes a simple (16) digit redemption code and instructions for first timers to redeem it. Once you receive the voucher, head over to Azteco and plug in the code and the deposit address for the voucher. Upon redemption, Azteco immediately writes the transaction to the mempool and within a couple blocks, the Bitcoin will arrive at the destination wallet.

Payment Methods

We currently accept payments on CashApp, Venmo, Paypal, and more to come. We are working to stand up new payment options such as money orders, PayPal invoicing, Square Terminal for Credit Card payments, and Walmart-2-Walmart payments.
